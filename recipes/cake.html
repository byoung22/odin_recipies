How to bake a Minecraft Cake

A delicious finished baked cake!

If you ever need a nice refreshing desert after a long battle, this savory cake has the perfect balance of a savory taste and magical healing abilities. Like seriously! How is this even possible? Its an ordinary cake with ordinary ingredients but it can heal you from a magical dragon attack? Can it cure cancer? Who truly knows?

Ingredients:

  • 3 Milk Buckets
  • 2 Scoops of Sugar
  • 3 Wheat bundles
  • 1 Egg

Steps:

  1. Turn on your trusty ol' crafting table
  2. Place the 3 Milks on the top row
  3. Place the 2 sugar on the second row in the corners
  4. Place the 3 wheat on the bottom of everything
  5. Slap a fat egg in the middle and craft the cake!
