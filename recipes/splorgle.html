How to whimple a Splorgle

Splorgle prep

After a nice successful hunt, a freshly whimpled splorgle for your tribial function is the best way to bond.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Fresh Splorgle
  • 2 Plog Claws
  • 4 Kradmew Eggs
  • A bowl of Homob Sauce

Steps:

  1. Cut your splorgle across the freege
  2. Skewer the feet with plog claws (gyrdle claws will work too)
  3. Harvest some homob sauce from your local splire farmer and drizzle it over the dish
  4. Add kradmew eggs as a final touch
